The concept is introduced

“负责任的消费与生产”——是联合国发布的“2030年人类未来议题”中确立课题，基于生产与消费的负面影响作出反省，人类各种不正确的消费欲望导致生物形态被改造。以荒诞的手法，批判看似文明社会发展的价值取向。
"Responsible consumption and production" is a topic established in the "2030 Agenda for the Future of Humanity" issued by the United Nations. It reflects on the negative impact of production and consumption. Various incorrect consumption desires of human beings lead to the transformation of biological forms. In an absurd way, it criticizes the value orientation of the development of seemingly civilized society
