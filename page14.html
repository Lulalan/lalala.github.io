Healing Illusion

或许你偶尔走在马路上会因为地上树木的影子而吓一大跳，以为是尾随着你的恶魔；或许你也会因为倒映在窗上外面楼房事物而吓一大跳，联想到张牙舞爪的怪物......
我想影子大概就是这么奇怪的存在，不看实体单看影子就能产生各种各样的联想。选用这个作为作品表现的形式也是因为影子给人带来的联想是足够满足“幻觉”这个主题的。但，对于我个人而言，面对着自然等一切美好的事物时是可以产生让我平静的感觉。我想，这大概就是我自我疗愈的一种方式，看着美好的事物总能让我心情愉悦。

而将想法与纸雕结合是因为想契合中华传统艺术—剪纸，从小对传统手艺感兴趣的我便想着将其结合。然而这最好的结合形式就是去打造不一样的走马灯。

You may be startled by the shadows of trees on the ground as you walk down the street, thinking that they are demons stalking you. Perhaps you will also be reflected in the window outside the building things and scared, think of the monster......

I think the shadow is probably such a strange existence, looking at the shadow without looking at the entity can produce all kinds of associations. This is also chosen as the form of expression of the work because the association brought by shadow is enough to satisfy the theme of "illusion". However, for me personally, facing nature and all good things can produce a sense of peace. I guess it's just my way of healing. Looking at something good always makes me feel good.

I combined my idea with paper carving because I wanted to fit the traditional Chinese art -- paper-cutting. Since I was young, I have been interested in traditional crafts and wanted to combine them. However, the best form of combination is to create a different lantern.
