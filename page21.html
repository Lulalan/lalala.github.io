污染与传染两个词（CONTAMINATION-CONTAGIOUS）都源于拉丁语的表示接触性质的单词。污染和传染必然意味着接触/保持联系，是接触、融合、共生的状态，是发展无法避免的趋势。污染带给我们积极的影响是让我们审视自己与自然的关系：人和污染相互连接的同时又相互影响，交融共生；这有助于我们采取一些积极措施去维持人与自然的平衡。我们希望通过这样沉浸式的交互装置引导人们思考，从发展的必然趋势中开创人与自然相处的新模式。
Both the words "CONTAMINATION and contagion" come from Latin words for the nature of contact. Contamination and infection necessarily mean contact/stay in contact, a state of contact, integration, symbiosis, an inevitable trend of development. The positive impact of pollution on us is that it makes us examine our relationship with nature. People and pollution are interconnected and influence each other, blending and symbiosis. This helps us to take some positive measures to maintain the balance between man and nature. Through such an immersive interactive device, we hope to guide people's thinking and create a new model for people to get along with nature from the inevitable trend of development.
