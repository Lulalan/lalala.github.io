传统意义的折纸是在二维平面纸面上运用翻、转、折等手法，创造出三维立体形态。剪裁、折叠甚至是粘合都会是结构的再造。它们外形别致且富有规律性的结构感，在我们手里，它存在无数的空间联想。正如电影《盗梦空间》里的筑梦师一般，我们可以增加自己的想法又或者带着各自独有的情怀。（或许空间、材料、颜色之间的运用能结合得更好。）我清楚感受到这不是感官上的错觉，而是真实存在的结构的改变，这个过程是我在再创造的过程中的运动的轨迹，或者说是维度转化的轨迹会更为合理？

The traditional sense of origami is to use the techniques of turning, turning and folding on two-dimensional flat paper to create a three-dimensional shape. Cutting, folding and even gluing are structural reconstructions. They are unique in appearance and have a sense of regular structure. In our hands, they have numerous spatial associations. Just like the dream architect in Inception, we can add our own ideas or bring our own. (Perhaps the use of space, material and color can be better combined.) I clearly feel that this is not a sensory illusion, but a real structural change. This process is the track of my movement in the process of re-creation, or the track of dimensional transformation.
