真实行为库
Real behavior library

在这一环节，我走出了校园，从人们不自觉的行为习惯出发；找到他们有意思的行为并引发思考，这些都是极其有意义的。我记录下他们的行为并编排真实行为库；

In this part, I go out of the campus, starting from people's unconscious behavior habits; Finding their interesting behavior and making them think is extremely meaningful. I record their behavior and compile a real behavior library;

Their behavior is full of contradictions and opposites, which makes me full of doubts, and I want to solve my doubts through further thinking.
